Simple Constitution Lessons

1 — What is a Constitution?

A constitution is a set of rules for how a country is run. It explains who makes decisions, what rights people have, and how leaders are chosen.

2 — Rights

Rights are things every person should have — like safety, a chance to go to school, and being treated fairly.

3 — Government and Leaders

The constitution shows how the government is organised and how leaders should behave. It helps people know who to ask when things go wrong.
