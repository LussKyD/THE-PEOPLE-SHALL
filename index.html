What is a Constitution?

A Constitution is the highest law of the land. It explains rights, leadership, and how the country is run.

Katiba ni sheria kuu. Inaelezea haki, uongozi, na jinsi nchi inavyoendeshwa.

Constitution Summary — Reader

Muhtasari wa Katiba — Msomaji

Official PDF (Parliament of Kenya)

Chapter One — Sovereignty of the People

English — Summary

This chapter confirms that ultimate power belongs to the Kenyan people. It explains how authority flows from citizens — through voting, public participation, and the rule of law. For youth, imagine your school elections as a tiny version of this idea: choosing class leaders is a way of practising how people choose representatives. For older generations, it’s a reminder that leaders answer to the people and must serve the public interest.

Kiswahili — Muhtasari

Sura hii inathibitisha kwamba madaraka ya juu yanatokana na watu wa Kenya. Inaelezea jinsi mamlaka inavyotokana na wananchi—kupitia kupiga kura, ushiriki wa umma, na utawala wa sheria. Kwa vijana, fikiria uchaguzi wa darasa kama toleo dogo la wazo hili: kuchagua viongozi wa darasa ni njia ya kuzoea jinsi watu wanavyoteua wawakilishi. Kwa wazee, ni ukumbusho kwamba viongozi wanawajibika kwa watu na wanapaswa kuhudumia maslahi ya umma.

Chapter Four — Bill of Rights

English — Summary

The Bill of Rights lists freedoms and protections for everyone — like equality, freedom of expression, and the right to education and healthcare. It’s central because it says what the state must protect. For young people this means the right to attend school, be safe, and speak up. Older generations should see it as the legal standard that protects vulnerable people and holds institutions to account.

Kiswahili — Muhtasari

Sura ya Haki za Msingi inaorodhesha uhuru na ulinzi kwa kila mtu—kama usawa, uhuru wa kujieleza, na haki ya elimu na huduma za afya. Ni muhimu kwa sababu inaelezea kile ambacho serikali inapaswa kulinda. Kwa vijana inamaanisha haki ya kwenda shuleni, kuwa salama, na kusema mawazo yako. Vizazi vikongwe vinapaswa kuiona kama viwango vya kisheria vinavyolinda watu walio katika hatari na kuwawajibisha taasisi.

Chapter Eight — Devolved Government

English — Summary

Devolution means sharing power with county governments. Counties manage local needs like health clinics, local roads, and markets. For young people, this shows how nearby leaders influence daily life — like fixing a school road or funding a clinic. For older generations, devolution is about bringing services closer and making government more responsive.

Kiswahili — Muhtasari

Ugatuzi ni kugawana mamlaka na serikali za kaunti. Kaunti zinashughulikia mahitaji ya karibu kama vituo vya afya, barabara za ndani, na masoko. Kwa vijana, inaonyesha jinsi viongozi wa karibu wanavyoathiri maisha ya kila siku—kama kukarabati barabara ya shule au kufadhili kituo cha afya. Kwa wazee, ugatuzi ni kuleta huduma karibu na watu na kufanya serikali iwe nyeti zaidi.

Schedules — Technical Details

English — Summary

Schedules are attachments to the Constitution that list details needed to apply the main chapters — like oath forms, transitional rules, and electoral details. Think of schedules like the appendix of a book: not for quick reading, but essential for officials and teachers who implement the law.

Kiswahili — Muhtasari

Ratiba ni nyongeza za Katiba ambazo zinaorodhesha maelezo yanayohitajika kutekeleza sura kuu—kama fomu za kiapo, sheria za mpito, na maelezo ya uchaguzi. Fikiria ratiba kama nyongeza ya kitabu: si kwa kusoma kwa haraka, lakini muhimu kwa watendaji na walimu wanaotekeleza sheria.
